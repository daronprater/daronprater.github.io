Welcome to Kenwood Advanced Dentistry

Practicing in Kenwood for over 20 years, Dr. Michael Prater and his staff are committed to providing you with the highest quality dental care in Cincinnati. He enjoys caring for his patients health and developing long-term relationships that allow him to provide exceptional, individualized care.

Dr. Prater and his staff perform a wide variety of dental services including implant placement, cosmetic restorations, and facial aesthetics. He is also certified by the American Academy of Facial Esthetics to administer botox and Dermal Filler treatments.
