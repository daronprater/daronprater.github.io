What is Periodontal Disease

Periodontal / Gum disease is a silent disease that usually begins without any symptoms. A large portion of the population has periodontal disease and don't realize they have it.

Periodontal disease is not only bad for your teeth and gums, but your overall health as well. It is important to not leave gum disease untreated.

How do you Know if You Have Periodontal Disease?

Symptoms of Periodontal Disease include:

  • Red and swollen gums
  • Loose teeth
  • Bleeding gums when brushing
  • Bad breath that won't go away
