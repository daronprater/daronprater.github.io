Tooth Whitening

Schedule an Appointment

Don't let the effects of tooth stains and discoloration diminish the quality of your smile! With professional teeth whitening, you can remove those pesky stains caused by coffee, tea and other products that leave your teeth stained.

Tooth Whitening Services

In your consultation, Dr. Prater will assess the overall health of your teeth and gums to confirm you are a good overall candidate for tooth whitening. Dr. Prater will then create a mold of your teeth to create a set of thin trays to use and apply the tooth whitening material. After approximately a month of tray use, a follow up consultation will occur with Dr. Prater to finalize your treatment.
