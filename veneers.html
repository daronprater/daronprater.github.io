Porcelain Veneers

Porcelain veneers are thin shells of ceramic that bond directly to the front and top surfaces of your teeth. When bonded to your teeth, porcelain veneers blend in beautifully with your natural smile and are highly resistant to tea and coffee stains.

Advantages of Porcelain Veeners


1. Improved Smile Appearance

Porcelain veeners are well suited for anyone with broken, discolored or misshapen teeth. The porcelain material bonds to your teeth which results in a natural beautiful smile.


2. Less Invasive than Crowns

The prodecure for porcelain veneer placement is much less invasive than that of crown placemenet and results in a stronger bond than traiditonal crowns.


3. Stain Resistant

Veneers tend to me more stain resistant to things like coffee and tea than traditional crowns.
